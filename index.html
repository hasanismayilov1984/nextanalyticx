Common Questions

Questions buyers actually ask

How long until we see a working dashboard?
Our standard commitment is a production-ready Power BI dashboard within 30 days of kickoff. We don't believe in 8-week discovery phases before any code ships. Discovery and the first build run in parallel.
How much does an engagement cost?
We work on hourly rates rather than padded fixed-fee projects. Most discovery + first-dashboard engagements land between $15,000 and $40,000 depending on data complexity. Larger Fabric migrations or AI integrations are scoped separately.
Our Power BI rollout failed. Can you fix it?
Yes — and it's a common engagement. Most failed rollouts come down to a weak semantic model or inconsistent metric definitions, not the tool itself. We start with an honest assessment and rebuild only what's necessary.
Do we need clean data before we start?
No. Clean data is something you get from the engagement — not something you need before it begins. We work with what exists, prioritize what matters, and build structure around real-world data.
Do you work with federal or government clients?
Yes. We hold a federal contracting registration and have delivered analytics work for US government clients. Our team is US-based (Northern Virginia), with permanent residents and US citizens — no offshore handoffs.
What's your stack expertise?
Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, OneLake, Azure, Databricks, Snowflake, dbt, Python, and SQL. On the AI side: Copilot in Fabric, RAG architectures, and OpenAI / Anthropic API integration.
